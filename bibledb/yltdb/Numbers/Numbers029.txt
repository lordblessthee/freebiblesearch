001 `And in the seventh month, in the first of the month, a holy convocation ye have, ye do no servile work; a day of shouting it is to you;  
002 and ye have prepared a burnt-offering, for sweet fragrance to Jehovah: one bullock, a son of the herd, one ram, seven lambs, sons of a year, perfect ones;  
003 and their present, flour mixed with oil, three-tenth deals for the bullock, two-tenth deals for the ram,  
004 and one-tenth deal for the one lamb, for the seven lambs;  
005 and one kid of the goats, a sin-offering, to make atonement for you;  
006 apart from the burnt-offering of the month, and its present, and the continual burnt-offering, and its present, and their libations, according to their ordinance, for sweet fragrance, a fire-offering to Jehovah.  
007 `And on the tenth of this seventh month a holy convocation ye have, and ye have humbled your souls; ye do no work;  
008 and ye have brought near a burnt-offering to Jehovah, a sweet fragrance, one bullock, a son of the herd, one ram, seven lambs, sons of a year, perfect ones they are for you,  
009 and their present, flour mixed with oil, three-tenth deals for the bullock, two-tenth deals for the one ram,  
010 a several tenth deal for the one lamb, for the seven lambs,  
011 one kid of the goats, a sin-offering; apart from the sin-offering of the atonements, and the continual burnt-offering, and its present, and their libations.  
012 `And on the fifteenth day of the seventh month a holy convocation ye have; ye do no servile work; and ye have celebrated a festival to Jehovah seven days,  
013 and have brought near a burnt-offering, a fire-offering, a sweet fragrance, to Jehovah; thirteen bullocks, sons of the herd, two rams, fourteen lambs, sons of a year; perfect ones they are;  
014 and their present, flour mixed with oil, three-tenth deals to the one bullock, for the thirteen bullocks, two-tenth deals to the one ram, for the two rams,  
015 and a several tenth deal to the one lamb, for the fourteen lambs,  
016 and one kid of the goats, a sin-offering; apart from the continual burnt-offering, its present, and its libation.  
017 `And on the second day twelve bullocks, sons of the herd, two rams, fourteen lambs, sons of a year, perfect ones;  
018 and their present, and their libations, for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the sheep, in their number, according to the ordinance;  
019 and one kid of the goats, a sin-offering; apart from the continual burnt-offering, and its present, and their libations.  
020 `And on the third day eleven bullocks, two rams, fourteen lambs, sons of a year, perfect ones;  
021 and their present, and their libations, for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, in their number, according to the ordinance;  
022 and one goat, a sin-offering; apart from the continual burnt-offering, and its present, and its libation.  
023 `And on the fourth day ten bullocks, two rams, fourteen lambs, sons of a year, perfect ones;  
024 their present, and their libations, for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, in their number, according to the ordinance;  
025 and one kid of the goats, a sin-offering, apart from the continual burnt-offering, its present, and its libation.  
026 `And on the fifth day nine bullocks, two rams, fourteen lambs, sons of a year, perfect ones;  
027 and their present, and their libations, for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, in their number, according to the ordinance;  
028 and one goat, a sin-offering; apart from the continual burnt-offering, and its present, and its libation.  
029 `And on the sixth day eight bullocks, two rams, fourteen lambs, sons of a year, perfect ones;  
030 and their present, and their libations, for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, in their number, according to the ordinance;  
031 and one goat, a sin-offering; apart from the continual burnt-offering, its present, and its libation.  
032 `And on the seventh day seven bullocks, two rams, fourteen lambs, sons of a year, perfect ones;  
033 and their present, and their libations, for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, in their number, according to the ordinance;  
034 and one goat, a sin-offering; apart from the continual burnt-offering, its present, and its libation.  
035 `On the eighth day a restraint ye have, ye do no servile work;  
036 and ye have brought near a burnt-offering, a fire-offering, a sweet fragrance, to Jehovah; one bullock, one ram, seven lambs, sons of a year, perfect ones;  
037 their present, and their libations, for the bullock, for the ram, and for the lambs, in their number, according to the ordinance;  
038 and one goat, a sin-offering; apart from the continual burnt-offering, and its present, and its libation.  
039 `These ye prepare to Jehovah in your appointed seasons, apart from your vows, and your free-will offerings, for your burnt-offerings, and for your presents, and for your libations, and for your peace-offerings.'  
040 And Moses saith unto the sons of Israel according to all that Jehovah hath commanded Moses.
