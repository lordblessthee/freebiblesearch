001 And these were sons of David, who were born to him in Hebron: the first-born Amnon, of Ahinoam the Jezreelitess; second Daniel, of Abigail the Carmelitess;  
002 the third Absalom, son of Maachah daughter of Talmai king of Geshur; the fourth Adonijah, son of Haggith;  
003 the fifth Shephatiah, of Abital; the sixth Ithream, of Eglah his wife.  
004 Six have been borne to him in Hebron, and he reigneth there seven years and six months, and thirty and three years he hath reigned in Jerusalem.  
005 And these were born to him in Jerusalem: Shimea, and Shobab, and Nathan, and Solomon -- four, of Bath-Sheba daughter of Ammiel:  
006 also Ibhar, and Elishama, and Eliphelet,  
007 and Nogah, and Nepheg, and Japhia,  
008 and Elishama, and Eliada, and Eliphelet, nine.  
009 All `are' sons of David, apart from sons of the concubines, and Tamar their sister. 
010 And the son of Solomon `is' Rehoboam, Abijah his son, Asa his son, Jehoshaphat his son, 
011 Joram his son, Ahaziah his son, Joash his son,  
012 Amaziah his son, Azariah his son, Jotham his son,  
013 Ahaz his son, Hezekiah his son, Manasseh his son,  
014 Amon his son, Josiah his son.  
015 And sons of Josiah: the first-born Johanan, the second Jehoiakim, the third Zedekiah, the fourth Shallum.  
016 And sons of Jehoiakim: Jeconiah his son, Zedekiah his son.  
017 And sons of Jeconiah: Assir; Salathiel his son;  
018 also Malchiram and Pedaiah, and Shenazzar, Jecamiah, Hoshama, and Nedabiah.  
019 And sons of Pedaiah: Zerubbabel, and Shimei. And sons of Zerubbabel: Meshullam, and Hananiah, and Shelomith their sister,  
020 and Hashubah, and Ohel, and Berechiah, and Hasadiah, Jushab-Hesed, five.  
021 And sons of Hananiah: Pelatiah, and Jesaiah, sons of Rephaiah, sons of Arnan, sons of Obadiah, sons of Shechaniah.  
022 And sons of Shechaniah: Shemaiah; and sons of Shemaiah: Hattush, and Igeal, and Bariah, and Neariah, and Shaphat, six.  
023 And sons of Neariah: Elioenai, and Hezekiah, and Azrikam, three.  
024 And sons of Elioenai: Hodaiah, and Eliashib, and Pelaiah, and Akkub, and Johanan, and Delaiah, and Anani, seven.
