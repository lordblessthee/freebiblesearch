001 Adam, Sheth, Enosh,  
002 Kenan, Mahalaleel, Jered,  
003 Henoch, Methuselah, Lamech,  
004 Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth.  
005 Sons of Japheth: Gomer and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras.  
006 And sons of Gomer: Ashchenaz, and Riphath, and Togarmah.  
007 And sons of Javan: Elisha, and Tarshishah, Kittim, and Dodanim.  
008 Sons of Ham: Cush, and Mizraim, Put, and Canaan.  
009 And sons of Cush: Seba and Havilah, and Sabta, and Raamah, and Sabtecka. And sons of Raamah: Sheba and Dedan.  
010 And Cush begat Nimrod: he began to be a mighty one in the land.  
011 And Mizraim begat the Ludim, and the Anamim, and the Lehabim, and the Naphtuhim,  
012 and the Pathrusim, and the Casluhim (from whom came out the Philistim), and the Caphtorim.  
013 And Canaan begat Zidon his first born, and Heth,  
014 and the Jebusite, and the Amorite, and the Girgashite,  
015 and the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite,  
016 and the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite.  
017 Sons of Shem: Elam and Asshur, and Arphaxad, and Lud, and Aram, and Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Meshech.  
018 And Arphaxad begat Shelah, and Shelah begat Eber.  
019 And to Eber have been born two sons, the name of the one `is' Peleg, for in his days hath the land been divided, and the name of his brother is Joktan. 
020 And Joktan begat Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah,  
021 and Hadoram, and Uzal, and Diklah,  
022 and Ebal, and Abimael, and Sheba,  
023 and Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab; all these `are' sons of Joktan. 
024 Shem, Arphaxad, Shelah,  
025 Eber, Peleg, Reu,  
026 Serug, Nahor, Terah,  
027 Abram -- he `is' Abraham. 
028 Sons of Abraham: Isaac and Ishmael.  
029 These `are' their generations: first-born of Ishmael, Nebaioth, and Kedar, and Adheel, and Mibsam, 
030 Mishma, and Dumah, Massa, Hadad, and Tema,  
031 Jetur, Naphish, and Kedema. These are sons of Ishmael.  
032 And sons of Keturah, Abraham's concubine: she bare Zimran, and Jokshan, and Medan, and Midian, and Ishbak, and Shuah. And sons of Jokshan: Sheba and Dedan.  
033 And sons of Midian: Ephah and Epher, and Henoch, and Abida, and Eldaah; all these `are' sons of Keturah. 
034 And Abraham begetteth Isaac. Sons of Isaac: Esau and Israel.  
035 Sons of Esau: Eliphaz, Reuel, and Jeush, and Jaalam, and Korah.  
036 Sons of Eliphaz: Teman, and Omar, Zephi, and Gatam, Kenaz, and Timna, and Amalek.  
037 Sons of Reuel: Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah.  
038 And sons of Seir: Lotan, and Shobal, and Zibeon, and Anah, and Dishon, and Ezar, and Dishan.  
039 And sons of Lotan: Hori and Homam, and sister of Lotan `is' Timna. 
040 Sons of Shobal: Alian, and Manahath, and Ebal, Shephi, and Onam. And sons of Zideon: Aiah and Anah.  
041 The sons of Anah: Dishon. and sons of Dishon: Amram, and Eshban, and Ithran, and Cheran.  
042 Sons of Ezer: Bilhan, and Zavan, Jakan. Sons of Dishan: Uz and Aran.  
043 And these `are' the kings who reigned in the land of Edom before the reigning of a king of the sons of Israel: Bela son of Beor, and the name of his city `is' Dinhabah. 
044 And Bela dieth, and reign in his stead doth Jobab son of Zerah from Bozrali;  
045 and Jobab dieth, and reign in his stead doth Husham from the land of the Temanite;  
046 and Husham dieth, and reign in his stead doth Hadad, son of Bedad (who smiteth Midian in the field of Moab) and the name of his city `is' Avith; 
047 and Hadad dieth, and reign in his stead doth Samlah from Masrekah;  
048 and Samlah dieth, and reign in his stead doth Shaul from Rehoboth of the River;  
049 and Shaul dieth, and reign in his stead doth Baal-Hanan son of Achbor;  
050 and Baal-Hanan dieth, and reign in his stead doth Hadad, and the name of his city `is' Pai, and the name of his wife `is' Mehetabel daughter of Matred, daughter of Me-Zahab; Hadad also dieth. 
051 And chiefs of Edom are: chief Timnah, chief Aliah, chief Jetheth,  
052 chief Aholibamah, chief Elah, chief Pinon,  
053 chief Kenaz, chief Teman, chief Mibzar,  
054 chief Magdiel, chief Iram. These `are' chiefs of Edom.
