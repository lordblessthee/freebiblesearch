001 And Benjamin begat Bela his first-born, Ashbel the second, and Aharah the third,  
002 Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth.  
003 And there are sons to Bela: Addar, and Gera,  
004 and Abihud, and Abishua, and Naaman, and Ahoah,  
005 and Gera, and Shephuphan, and Huram.  
006 And these `are' sons of Ehud: they are heads of fathers to the inhabitants of Geba, and they remove them unto Manahath; 
007 and Naaman, and Ahiah, and Gera, he removed them, and begat Uzza and Ahihud.  
008 And Shaharaim begat in the field of Moab, after his sending them away; Hushim and Baara `are' his wives. 
009 And he begetteth of Hodesh his wife, Jobab, and Zibia, and Mesha, and Malcham,  
010 and Jeuz, and Shachiah, and Mirmah. These `are' his sons, heads of fathers. 
011 And of Hushim he begat Ahitub and Elpaal.  
012 And sons of Elpaal: Eber, and Misheam, and Shamer, (he built Ono and Lod and its small towns),  
013 and Beriah and Shema, (they `are' the heads of fathers to the inhabitants of Aijalon -- they caused to flee the inhabitants of Gath), 
014 and Ahio, Shashak, and Jeremoth,  
015 and Zebadiah, and Arad, and Ader,  
016 and Michael, and Ispah, and Joha, sons of Beriah,  
017 and Zebadiah, and Meshullam, and Hezeki, and Heber,  
018 and Ishmerai, and Jezliah, and Jobab, sons of Elpaal;  
019 And Jakim, and Zichri, and Zabdi,  
020 and Elienai, and Zillethai, and Eliel,  
021 and Adaiah, and Beraiah, and Shimrath, sons of Shimei;  
022 And Ishpan, and Heber, and Eliel,  
023 and Abdon, and Zichri, and Hanan,  
024 and Hananiah, and Elam, and Antothijah,  
025 and Iphedeiah, and Penuel, sons of Shashak;  
026 And Shamsherai, and Shehariah, and Athaliah,  
027 and Jaareshiah, and Eliah, and Zichri, sons of Jeroham.  
028 These `are' heads of fathers, by their generations, heads; these dwelt in Jerusalem. 
029 And in Gibeon hath the father of Gibeon dwelt, and the name of his wife `is' Maachah; 
030 and his son, the first-born, `is' Abdon, and Zur, and Kish, and Baal, and Nadab, 
031 and Gedor, and Ahio, and Zacher;  
032 and Mikloth begat Shimeah. And they also over-against their brethren dwelt in Jerusalem with their brethren.  
033 And Ner begat Kish, and Kish begat Saul, and Saul begat Jonathan, and Malchi-Shua, and Abinadab, and Esh-Baal.  
034 And a son of Jonathan `is' Merib-Baal, and Merib-Baal begat Micah; 
035 and sons of Micah: Pithon, and Melech, and Tarea, and Ahaz:  
036 and Ahaz begat Jehoadah, and Jehoadah begat Alemeth, and Azmaveth, and Zimri; and Zimri begat Moza,  
037 and Moza begat Binea, Raphah `is' his son, Eleasah his son, Azel his son. 
038 And to Azel `are' six sons, and these `are' their names: Azrikam, Bocheru, and Ishmael, and Sheariah, and Obadiah, and Hanan. All these `are' sons of Azel. 
039 And sons of Eshek his brother: Ulam his first-born, Jehush the second, and Eliphelet the third.  
040 And the sons of Ulam are men mighty in valour, treading bow, and multiplying sons and son's sons, a hundred and fifty. All these `are' of the sons of Benjamin.
