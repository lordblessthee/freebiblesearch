001 Now Benjamin begat Bela his firstborn, Ashbel the second, and Aharah the third, 
002 Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth. 
003 And the sons of Bela were, Addar, and Gera, and Abihud, 
004 And Abishua, and Naaman, and Ahoah, 
005 And Gera, and Shephuphan, and Huram. 
006 And these are the sons of Ehud: these are the heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Geba, and they removed them to Manahath: 
007 And Naaman, and Ahiah, and Gera, he removed them, and begat Uzza, and Ahihud. 
008 And Shaharaim begat children in the country of Moab, after he had sent them away; Hushim and Baara were his wives. 
009 And he begat of Hodesh his wife, Jobab, and Zibia, and Mesha, and Malcham, 
010 And Jeuz, and Shachia, and Mirma. These were his sons, heads of the fathers. 
011 And of Hushim he begat Abitub, and Elpaal. 
012 The sons of Elpaal; Eber, and Misham, and Shamed, who built Ono, and Lod, with the towns thereof: 
013 Beriah also, and Shema, who were heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Aijalon, who drove away the inhabitants of Gath: 
014 And Ahio, Shashak, and Jeremoth, 
015 And Zebadiah, and Arad, and Ader, 
016 And Michael, and Ispah, and Joha, the sons of Beriah; 
017 And Zebadiah, and Meshullam, and Hezeki, and Heber, 
018 Ishmerai also, and Jezliah, and Jobab, the sons of Elpaal; 
019 And Jakim, and Zichri, and Zabdi, 
020 And Elienai, and Zilthai, and Eliel, 
021 And Adaiah, and Beraiah, and Shimrath, the sons of Shimhi; 
022 And Ishpan, and Heber, and Eliel, 
023 And Abdon, and Zichri, and Hanan, 
024 And Hananiah, and Elam, and Antothijah, 
025 And Iphedeiah, and Penuel, the sons of Shashak; 
026 And Shamsherai, and Shehariah, and Athaliah, 
027 And Jaresiah, and Eliah, and Zichri, the sons of Jeroham. 
028 These were heads of the fathers, by their generations, chief men. These dwelt in Jerusalem. 
029 And at Gibeon dwelt the father of Gibeon; whose wife's name was Maachah: 
030 And his firstborn son Abdon, and Zur, and Kish, and Baal, and Nadab, 
031 And Gedor, and Ahio, and Zacher. 
032 And Mikloth begat Shimeah. And these also dwelt with their brethren in Jerusalem, over against them. 
033 And Ner begat Kish, and Kish begat Saul, and Saul begat Jonathan, and Malchishua, and Abinadab, and Eshbaal. 
034 And the son of Jonathan was Meribbaal; and Meribbaal begat Micah. 
035 And the sons of Micah were, Pithon, and Melech, and Tarea, and Ahaz. 
036 And Ahaz begat Jehoadah; and Jehoadah begat Alemeth, and Azmaveth, and Zimri; and Zimri begat Moza, 
037 And Moza begat Binea: Rapha was his son, Eleasah his son, Azel his son: 
038 And Azel had six sons, whose names are these, Azrikam, Bocheru, and Ishmael, and Sheariah, and Obadiah, and Hanan. All these were the sons of Azel. 
039 And the sons of Eshek his brother were, Ulam his firstborn, Jehush the second, and Eliphelet the third. 
040 And the sons of Ulam were mighty men of valour, archers, and had many sons, and sons' sons, an hundred and fifty. All these are of the sons of Benjamin.
