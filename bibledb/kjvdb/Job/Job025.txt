001 Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said, 
002 Dominion and fear are with him, he maketh peace in his high places. 
003 Is there any number of his armies? and upon whom doth not his light arise? 
004 How then can man be justified with God? or how can he be clean that is born of a woman? 
005 Behold even to the moon, and it shineth not; yea, the stars are not pure in his sight. 
006 How much less man, that is a worm? and the son of man, which is a worm?
