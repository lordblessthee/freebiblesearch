001 This is the book of the generations of Adam. In the day that God created man, in the likeness of God made he him; 
002 Male and female created he them; and blessed them, and called their name Adam, in the day when they were created. 
003 And Adam lived an hundred and thirty years, and begat a son in his own likeness, after his image; and called his name Seth: 
004 And the days of Adam after he had begotten Seth were eight hundred years: and he begat sons and daughters: 
005 And all the days that Adam lived were nine hundred and thirty years: and he died. 
006 And Seth lived an hundred and five years, and begat Enos: 
007 And Seth lived after he begat Enos eight hundred and seven years, and begat sons and daughters: 
008 And all the days of Seth were nine hundred and twelve years: and he died. 
009 And Enos lived ninety years, and begat Cainan: 
010 And Enos lived after he begat Cainan eight hundred and fifteen years, and begat sons and daughters: 
011 And all the days of Enos were nine hundred and five years: and he died. 
012 And Cainan lived seventy years, and begat Mahalaleel: 
013 And Cainan lived after he begat Mahalaleel eight hundred and forty years, and begat sons and daughters: 
014 And all the days of Cainan were nine hundred and ten years: and he died. 
015 And Mahalaleel lived sixty and five years, and begat Jared: 
016 And Mahalaleel lived after he begat Jared eight hundred and thirty years, and begat sons and daughters: 
017 And all the days of Mahalaleel were eight hundred ninety and five years: and he died. 
018 And Jared lived an hundred sixty and two years, and he begat Enoch: 
019 And Jared lived after he begat Enoch eight hundred years, and begat sons and daughters: 
020 And all the days of Jared were nine hundred sixty and two years: and he died. 
021 And Enoch lived sixty and five years, and begat Methuselah: 
022 And Enoch walked with God after he begat Methuselah three hundred years, and begat sons and daughters: 
023 And all the days of Enoch were three hundred sixty and five years: 
024 And Enoch walked with God: and he was not; for God took him. 
025 And Methuselah lived an hundred eighty and seven years, and begat Lamech: 
026 And Methuselah lived after he begat Lamech seven hundred eighty and two years, and begat sons and daughters: 
027 And all the days of Methuselah were nine hundred sixty and nine years: and he died. 
028 And Lamech lived an hundred eighty and two years, and begat a son: 
029 And he called his name Noah, saying, This same shall comfort us concerning our work and toil of our hands, because of the ground which the LORD hath cursed. 
030 And Lamech lived after he begat Noah five hundred ninety and five years, and begat sons and daughters: 
031 And all the days of Lamech were seven hundred seventy and seven years: and he died. 
032 And Noah was five hundred years old: and Noah begat Shem, Ham, and Japheth.
