001 O give thanks unto the LORD; for he is good: for his mercy endureth for ever. 
002 O give thanks unto the God of gods: for his mercy endureth for ever. 
003 O give thanks to the Lord of lords: for his mercy endureth for ever. 
004 To him who alone doeth great wonders: for his mercy endureth for ever. 
005 To him that by wisdom made the heavens: for his mercy endureth for ever. 
006 To him that stretched out the earth above the waters: for his mercy endureth for ever. 
007 To him that made great lights: for his mercy endureth for ever: 
008 The sun to rule by day: for his mercy endureth for ever: 
009 The moon and stars to rule by night: for his mercy endureth for ever. 
010 To him that smote Egypt in their firstborn: for his mercy endureth for ever: 
011 And brought out Israel from among them: for his mercy endureth for ever: 
012 With a strong hand, and with a stretched out arm: for his mercy endureth for ever. 
013 To him which divided the Red sea into parts: for his mercy endureth for ever: 
014 And made Israel to pass through the midst of it: for his mercy endureth for ever: 
015 But overthrew Pharaoh and his host in the Red sea: for his mercy endureth for ever. 
016 To him which led his people through the wilderness: for his mercy endureth for ever. 
017 To him which smote great kings: for his mercy endureth for ever: 
018 And slew famous kings: for his mercy endureth for ever: 
019 Sihon king of the Amorites: for his mercy endureth for ever: 
020 And Og the king of Bashan: for his mercy endureth for ever: 
021 And gave their land for an heritage: for his mercy endureth for ever: 
022 Even an heritage unto Israel his servant: for his mercy endureth for ever. 
023 Who remembered us in our low estate: for his mercy endureth for ever: 
024 And hath redeemed us from our enemies: for his mercy endureth for ever. 
025 Who giveth food to all flesh: for his mercy endureth for ever. 
026 O give thanks unto the God of heaven: for his mercy endureth for ever.
