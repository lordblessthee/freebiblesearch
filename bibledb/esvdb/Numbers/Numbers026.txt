001 After the plague, the LORD said to Moses and to Eleazar the son of Aaron, the priest, 
002 `Take a census of all the congregation of the people of Israel, from twenty years old and upward, by their fathers' houses, all in Israel who are able to go to war.` 
003 And Moses and Eleazar the priest spoke with them in the plains of Moab by the Jordan at Jericho, saying, 
004 `Take a census of the people, from twenty years old and upward,` as the LORD commanded Moses. The people of Israel who came out of the land of Egypt were: 
005 Reuben, the firstborn of Israel; the sons of Reuben: of Hanoch, the clan of the Hanochites; of Pallu, the clan of the Palluites; 
006 of Hezron, the clan of the Hezronites; of Carmi, the clan of the Carmites. 
007 These are the clans of the Reubenites, and those listed were 43,730. 
008 And the sons of Pallu: Eliab. 
009 The sons of Eliab: Nemuel, Dathan, and Abiram. These are the Dathan and Abiram, chosen from the congregation, who contended against Moses and Aaron in the company of Korah, when they contended against the LORD 
010 and the earth opened its mouth and swallowed them up together with Korah, when that company died, when the fire devoured 250 men, and they became a warning. 
011 But the sons of Korah did not die. 
012 The sons of Simeon according to their clans: of Nemuel, the clan of the Nemuelites; of Jamin, the clan of the Ja-minites; of Jachin, the clan of the Jachinites; 
013 of Zerah, the clan of the Zerahites; of Shaul, the clan of the Sha-ulites. 
014 These are the clans of the Simeonites, 22,200. 
015 The sons of Gad according to their clans: of Zephon, the clan of the Zephonites; of Haggi, the clan of the Haggites; of Shuni, the clan of the Shunites; 
016 of Ozni, the clan of the Oznites; of Eri, the clan of the Erites; 
017 of Arod, the clan of the Arodites; of Areli, the clan of the Arelites. 
018 These are the clans of the sons of Gad as they were listed, 40,500. 
019 The sons of Judah were Er and Onan; and Er and Onan died in the land of Canaan. 
020 And the sons of Judah according to their clans were: of Shelah, the clan of the Shelanites; of Perez, the clan of the Perezites; of Zerah, the clan of the Zerahites. 
021 And the sons of Perez were: of Hezron, the clan of the Hezronites; of Hamul, the clan of the Hamulites. 
022 These are the clans of Judah as they were listed, 76,500. 
023 The sons of Issachar according to their clans: of Tola, the clan of the Tolaites; of Puvah, the clan of the Punites; 
024 of Jashub, the clan of the Ja-shubites; of Shimron, the clan of the Shimronites. 
025 These are the clans of Issachar as they were listed, 64,300. 
026 The sons of Zebulun, according to their clans: of Sered, the clan of the Seredites; of Elon, the clan of the Elonites; of Jahleel, the clan of the Jah-leelites. 
027 These are the clans of the Zeb-ulunites as they were listed, 60,500. 
028 The sons of Joseph according to their clans: Manasseh and Ephraim. 
029 The sons of Manasseh: of Machir, the clan of the Machirites; and Machir was the father of Gilead; of Gilead, the clan of the Gileadites. 
030 These are the sons of Gilead: of Iezer, the clan of the Iezerites; of Helek, the clan of the Helekites; 
031 and of Asriel, the clan of the Asrielites; and of Shechem, the clan of the Shechemites; 
032 and of Shemida, the clan of the Shemidaites; and of Hepher, the clan of the Hepherites. 
033 Now Zelophehad the son of Hepher had no sons, but daughters. And the names of the daughters of Zelophehad were Mahlah, Noah, Hoglah, Milcah, and Tirzah. 
034 These are the clans of Manasseh, and those listed were 52,700. 
035 These are the sons of Ephraim according to their clans: of Shuthelah, the clan of the Shuthelahites; of Becher, the clan of the Becherites; of Tahan, the clan of the Tahanites. 
036 And these are the sons of Shuthelah: of Eran, the clan of the Eranites. 
037 These are the clans of the sons of Ephraim as they were listed, 32,500. These are the sons of Joseph according to their clans. 
038 The sons of Benjamin according to their clans: of Bela, the clan of the Belaites; of Ashbel, the clan of the Ashbelites; of Ahiram, the clan of the Ahiramites; 
039 of Shephupham, the clan of the Shuphamites; of Hupham, the clan of the Huphamites. 
040 And the sons of Bela were Ard and Naaman: of Ard, the clan of the Ardites; of Naaman, the clan of the Naamites. 
041 These are the sons of Benjamin according to their clans, and those listed were 45,600. 
042 These are the sons of Dan according to their clans: of Shuham, the clan of the Shuhamites. These are the clans of Dan according to their clans. 
043 All the clans of the Shuhamites, as they were listed, were 64,400. 
044 The sons of Asher according to their clans: of Imnah, the clan of the Imnites; of Ishvi, the clan of the Ishvites; of Beriah, the clan of the Beriites. 
045 Of the sons of Beriah: of Heber, the clan of the Heberites; of Malchiel, the clan of the Malchielites. 
046 And the name of the daughter of Asher was Serah. 
047 These are the clans of the sons of Asher as they were listed, 53,400. 
048 The sons of Naphtali according to their clans: of Jahzeel, the clan of the Jahzeelites; of Guni, the clan of the Gunites; 
049 of Jezer, the clan of the Jezerites; of Shillem, the clan of the Shillemites. 
050 These are the clans of Naphtali according to their clans, and those listed were 45,400. 
051 This was the list of the people of Israel, 601,730. 
052 The LORD spoke to Moses, saying, 
053 `Among these the land shall be divided for inheritance according to the number of names. 
054 To a large tribe you shall give a large inheritance, and to a small tribe you shall give a small inheritance; every tribe shall be given its inheritance in proportion to its list. 
055 But the land shall be divided by lot. According to the names of the tribes of their fathers they shall inherit. 
056 Their inheritance shall be divided according to lot between the larger and the smaller.` 
057 This was the list of the Levites according to their clans: of Gershon, the clan of the Gershonites; of Kohath, the clan of the Kohathites; of Merari, the clan of the Merarites. 
058 These are the clans of Levi: the clan of the Libnites, the clan of the Hebronites, the clan of the Mahlites, the clan of the Mushites, the clan of the Korahites. And Kohath was the father of Amram. 
059 The name of Amram's wife was Jochebed the daughter of Levi, who was born to Levi in Egypt. And she bore to Amram Aaron and Moses and Miriam their sister. 
060 And to Aaron were born Nadab, Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar. 
061 But Nadab and Abihu died when they offered unauthorized fire before the LORD. 
062 And those listed were 23,000, every male from a month old and upward. For they were not listed among the people of Israel, because there was no inheritance given to them among the people of Israel. 
063 These were those listed by Moses and Eleazar the priest, who listed the people of Israel in the plains of Moab by the Jordan at Jericho. 
064 But among these there was not one of those listed by Moses and Aaron the priest, who had listed the people of Israel in the wilderness of Sinai. 
065 For the LORD had said of them, `They shall die in the wilderness.` Not one of them was left, except Caleb the son of Jephunneh and Joshua the son of Nun.
