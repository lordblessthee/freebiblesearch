001 On the day when Moses had finished setting up the tabernacle and had anointed and consecrated it with all its furnishings and had anointed and consecrated the altar with all its utensils, 
002 the chiefs of Israel, heads of their fathers' houses, who were the chiefs of the tribes, who were over those who were listed, approached 
003 and brought their offerings before the LORD, six wagons and twelve oxen, a wagon for every two of the chiefs, and for each one an ox. They brought them before the tabernacle. 
004 Then the LORD said to Moses, 
005 `Accept these from them, that they may be used in the service of the tent of meeting, and give them to the Levites, to each man according to his service.` 
006 So Moses took the wagons and the oxen and gave them to the Levites. 
007 Two wagons and four oxen he gave to the sons of Gershon, according to their service. 
008 And four wagons and eight oxen he gave to the sons of Merari, according to their service, under the direction of Ithamar the son of Aaron the priest. 
009 But to the sons of Kohath he gave none, because they were charged with the service of the holy things that had to be carried on the shoulder. 
010 And the chiefs offered offerings for the dedication of the altar on the day it was anointed; and the chiefs offered their offering before the altar. 
011 And the LORD said to Moses, `They shall offer their offerings, one chief each day, for the dedication of the altar.` 
012 He who offered his offering the first day was Nahshon the son of Amminadab, of the tribe of Judah. 
013 And his offering was one silver plate whose weight was 130 shekels, one silver basin of 70 shekels, according to the shekel of the sanctuary, both of them full of fine flour mixed with oil for a grain offering; 
014 one golden dish of 10 shekels, full of incense; 
015 one bull from the herd, one ram, one male lamb a year old, for a burnt offering; 
016 one male goat for a sin offering; 
017 and for the sacrifice of peace offerings, two oxen, five rams, five male goats, and five male lambs a year old. This was the offering of Nahshon the son of Amminadab.

084 This was the dedication offering for the altar on the day when it was anointed, from the chiefs of Israel: twelve silver plates, twelve silver basins, twelve golden dishes, 
085 each silver plate weighing 130 shekels and each basin 70, all the silver of the vessels 2,400 shekels according to the shekel of the sanctuary, 
086 the twelve golden dishes, full of incense, weighing 10 shekels apiece according to the shekel of the sanctuary, all the gold of the dishes being 120 shekels; 
087 all the cattle for the burnt offering twelve bulls, twelve rams, twelve male lambs a year old, with their grain offering; and twelve male goats for a sin offering; 
088 and all the cattle for the sacrifice of peace offerings twenty-four bulls, the rams sixty, the male goats sixty, the male lambs a year old sixty. This was the dedication offering for the altar after it was anointed. 
089 And when Moses went into the tent of meeting to speak with the LORD, he heard the voice speaking to him from above the mercy seat that was on the ark of the testimony, from between the two cherubim; and it spoke to him.
