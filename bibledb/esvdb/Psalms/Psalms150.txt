001 Praise the LORD!Praise God in his sanctuary; praise him in his mighty heavens! 
002 Praise him for his mighty deeds; praise him according to his excellent greatness! 
003 Praise him with trumpet sound; praise him with lute and harp! 
004 Praise him with tambourine and dance; praise him with strings and pipe! 
005 Praise him with sounding cymbals; praise him with loud clashing cymbals! 
006 Let everything that has breath praise the LORD! Praise the LORD!
