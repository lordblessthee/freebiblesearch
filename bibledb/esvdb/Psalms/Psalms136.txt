001 Give thanks to the LORD, for he is good, for his steadfast love endures forever. 
002 Give thanks to the God of gods, for his steadfast love endures forever. 
003 Give thanks to the Lord of lords, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
004 to him who alone does great wonders, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
005 to him who by understanding made the heavens, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
006 to him who spread out the earth above the waters, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
007 to him who made the great lights, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
008 the sun to rule over the day, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
009 the moon and stars to rule over the night, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
010 to him who struck down the firstborn of Egypt, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
011 and brought Israel out from among them, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
012 with a strong hand and an outstretched arm, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
013 to him who divided the Red Sea in two, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
014 and made Israel pass through the midst of it, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
015 but overthrew Pharaoh and his host in the Red Sea, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
016 to him who led his people through the wilderness, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
017 to him who struck down great kings, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
018 and killed mighty kings, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
019 Sihon, king of the Amorites, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
020 and Og, king of Bashan, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
021 and gave their land as a heritage, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
022 a heritage to Israel his servant, for his steadfast love endures forever. 
023 It is he who remembered us in our low estate, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
024 and rescued us from our foes, for his steadfast love endures forever; 
025 he who gives food to all flesh, for his steadfast love endures forever. 
026 Give thanks to the God of heaven, for his steadfast love endures forever.
