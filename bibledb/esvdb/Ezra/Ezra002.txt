061 Also, of the sons of the priests: the sons of Habaiah, the sons of Hakkoz, and the sons of Barzillai (who had taken a wife from the daughters of Barzillai the Gileadite, and was called by their name). 
062 These sought their registration among those enrolled in the genealogies, but they were not found there, and so they were excluded from the priesthood as unclean. 
063 The governor told them that they were not to partake of the most holy food, until there should be a priest to consult Urim and Thummim. 
064 The whole assembly together was 42,360, 
065 besides their male and female servants, of whom there were 7,337, and they had 200 male and female singers. 
066 Their horses were 736, their mules were 245, 
067 their camels were 435, and their donkeys were 6,720. 
068 Some of the heads of families, when they came to the house of the LORD that is in Jerusalem, made freewill offerings for the house of God, to erect it on its site. 
069 According to their ability they gave to the treasury of the work 61,000 darics of gold, 5,000 minas of silver, and 100 priests' garments. 
070 Now the priests, the Levites, some of the people, the singers, the gatekeepers, and the temple servants lived in their towns, and all the rest of Israel in their towns.
