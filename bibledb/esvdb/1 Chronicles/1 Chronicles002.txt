001 These are the sons of Israel: Reuben, Simeon, Levi, Judah, Issachar, Zebulun, 
002 Dan, Joseph, Benjamin, Naphtali, Gad, and Asher. 
003 The sons of Judah: Er, Onan and Shelah; these three Bath-shua the Canaanite bore to him. Now Er, Judah's firstborn, was evil in the sight of the LORD, and he put him to death. 
004 His daughter-in-law Tamar also bore him Perez and Zerah. Judah had five sons in all. 
005 The sons of Perez: Hezron and Hamul. 
006 The sons of Zerah: Zimri, Ethan, Heman, Calcol, and Dara, five in all. 
007 The son of Carmi: Achan, the troubler of Israel, who broke faith in the matter of the devoted thing; 
008 and Ethan's son was Azariah. 
009 The sons of Hezron that were born to him: Jerahmeel, Ram, and Chelubai. 
010 Ram fathered Amminadab, and Amminadab fathered Nahshon, prince of the sons of Judah. 
011 Nahshon fathered Salmon, Salmon fathered Boaz, 
012 Boaz fathered Obed, Obed fathered Jesse. 
013 Jesse fathered Eliab his firstborn, Abinadab the second, Shimea the third, 
014 Nethanel the fourth, Raddai the fifth, 
015 Ozem the sixth, David the seventh. 
016 And their sisters were Zeruiah and Abigail. The sons of Zeruiah: Abishai, Joab, and Asahel, three. 
017 Abigail bore Amasa, and the father of Amasa was Jether the Ishmaelite. 
018 Caleb the son of Hezron fathered children by his wife Azubah, and by Jerioth; and these were her sons: Jesher, Shobab, and Ardon. 
019 When Azubah died, Caleb married Ephrath, who bore him Hur. 
020 Hur fathered Uri, and Uri fathered Bezalel. 
021 Afterward Hezron went in to the daughter of Machir the father of Gilead, whom he married when he was sixty years old, and she bore him Segub. 
022 And Segub fathered Jair, who had twenty-three cities in the land of Gilead. 
023 But Geshur and Aram took from them Havvoth-jair, Kenath, and its villages, sixty towns. All these were descendants of Machir, the father of Gilead. 
024 After the death of Hezron, Caleb went in to Ephrathah, the wife of Hezron his father, and she bore him Ashhur, the father of Tekoa. 
025 The sons of Jerahmeel, the firstborn of Hezron: Ram, his firstborn, Bunah, Oren, Ozem, and Ahijah. 
026 Jerahmeel also had another wife, whose name was Atarah; she was the mother of Onam. 
027 The sons of Ram, the firstborn of Jerahmeel: Maaz, Jamin, and Eker. 
028 The sons of Onam: Shammai and Jada. The sons of Shammai: Nadab and Abishur. 
029 The name of Abishur's wife was Abihail, and she bore him Ahban and Molid. 
030 The sons of Nadab: Seled and Appaim; and Seled died childless. 
031 The son of Appaim: Ishi. The son of Ishi: Sheshan. The son of Sheshan: Ahlai. 
032 The sons of Jada, Shammai's brother: Jether and Jonathan; and Jether died childless. 
033 The sons of Jonathan: Peleth and Zaza. These were the descendants of Jerahmeel. 
034 Now Sheshan had no sons, only daughters, but Sheshan had an Egyptian slave whose name was Jarha. 
035 So Sheshan gave his daughter in marriage to Jarha his slave, and she bore him Attai. 
036 Attai fathered Nathan, and Nathan fathered Zabad. 
037 Zabad fathered Ephlal, and Ephlal fathered Obed. 
038 Obed fathered Jehu, and Jehu fathered Azariah. 
039 Azariah fathered Helez, and Helez fathered Eleasah. 
040 Eleasah fathered Sismai, and Sismai fathered Shallum. 
041 Shallum fathered Jekamiah, and Jekamiah fathered Elishama. 
042 The sons of Caleb the brother of Jerahmeel: Mareshah his firstborn, who fathered Ziph. The son of Mareshah: Hebron. 
043 The sons of Hebron: Korah, Tappuah, Rekem and Shema. 
044 Shema fathered Raham, the father of Jorkeam; and Rekem fathered Shammai. 
045 The son of Shammai: Maon; and Maon fathered Beth-zur. 
046 Ephah also, Caleb's concubine, bore Haran, Moza, and Gazez; and Haran fathered Gazez. 
047 The sons of Jahdai: Regem, Jotham, Geshan, Pelet, Ephah, and Shaaph. 
048 Maacah, Caleb's concubine, bore Sheber and Tirhanah. 
049 She also bore Shaaph the father of Madmannah, Sheva the father of Machbenah and the father of Gibea; and the daughter of Caleb was Achsah. 
050 These were the descendants of Caleb. The sons of Hur the firstborn of Ephrathah: Shobal the father of Kiriath-jearim, 
051 Salma, the father of Bethlehem, and Hareph the father of Beth-gader. 
052 Shobal the father of Kiriath-jearim had other sons: Haroeh, half of the Menuhoth. 
053 And the clans of Kiriath-jearim: the Ithrites, the Puthites, the Shumathites, and the Mishraites; from these came the Zorathites and the Eshtaolites. 
054 The sons of Salma: Bethlehem, the Netophathites, Atroth-beth-joab and half of the Manahathites, the Zorites. 
055 The clans also of the scribes who lived at Jabez: the Tirathites, the Shimeathites and the Sucathites. These are the Kenites who came from Hammath, the father of the house of Rechab.
