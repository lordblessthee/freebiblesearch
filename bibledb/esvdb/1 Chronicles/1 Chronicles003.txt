001 These are the sons of David who were born to him in Hebron: the firstborn, Amnon, by Ahinoam the Jezreelite; the second, Daniel, by Abigail the Carmelite, 
002 the third, Absalom, whose mother was Maacah, the daughter of Talmai, king of Geshur; the fourth, Adonijah, whose mother was Haggith; 
003 the fifth, Shephatiah, by Abital; the sixth, Ithream, by his wife Eglah; 
004 six were born to him in Hebron, where he reigned for seven years and six months. And he reigned thirty-three years in Jerusalem. 
005 These were born to him in Jerusalem: Shimea, Shobab, Nathan and Solomon, four by Bath-shua, the daughter of Ammiel; 
006 then Ibhar, Elishama, Eliphelet, 
007 Nogah, Nepheg, Japhia, 
008 Elishama, Eliada, and Eliphelet, nine. 
009 All these were David's sons, besides the sons of the concubines, and Tamar was their sister. 
010 The son of Solomon was Rehoboam, Abijah his son, Asa his son, Jehoshaphat his son, 
011 Joram his son, Ahaziah his son, Joash his son, 
012 Amaziah his son, Azariah his son, Jotham his son, 
013 Ahaz his son, Hezekiah his son, Manasseh his son, 
014 Amon his son, Josiah his son. 
015 The sons of Josiah: Johanan the firstborn, the second Jehoiakim, the third Zedekiah, the fourth Shallum. 
016 The descendants of Jehoiakim: Jeconiah his son, Zedekiah his son; 
017 and the sons of Jeconiah, the captive: Shealtiel his son, 
018 Malchiram, Pedaiah, Shenazzar, Jekamiah, Hosh-ama and Nedabiah; 
019 and the sons of Pedaiah: Zerubbabel and Shimei; and the sons of Zerubbabel: Meshullam and Hananiah, and Shelomith was their sister; 
020 and Hashubah, Ohel, Berechiah, Hasadiah, and Jushab-hesed, five. 
021 The sons of Hananiah: Pelatiah and Jeshaiah, his son Rephaiah, his son Arnan, his son Obadiah, his son Shecaniah. 
022 The son of Shecaniah: Shemaiah. And the sons of Shemaiah: Hattush, Igal, Bariah, Neariah, and Shaphat, six. 
023 The sons of Neariah: Elioenai, Hizkiah, and Azrikam, three. 
024 The sons of Elioenai: Hodaviah, Eliashib, Pelaiah, Akkub, Johanan, Delaiah, and Anani, seven.
