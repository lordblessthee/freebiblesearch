001 Adam, Seth, Enosh; 
002 Kenan, Mahalalel, Jared; 
003 Enoch, Methuselah, Lamech; 
004 Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth. 
005 The sons of Japheth: Gomer, Magog, Madai, Javan, Tubal, Meshech, and Tiras. 
006 The sons of Gomer: Ashkenaz, Riphath, and Togarmah. 
007 The sons of Javan: Elishah, Tarshish, Kittim, and Rodanim. 
008 The sons of Ham: Cush, Egypt, Put, and Canaan. 
009 The sons of Cush: Seba, Havilah, Sabta, Raama, and Sabteca. The sons of Raamah: Sheba and Dedan. 
010 Cush fathered Nimrod. He was the first on earth to be a mighty man. 
011 Egypt fathered Ludim, Anamim, Lehabim, Naphtuhim, 
012 Pathrusim, Casluhim (from whom the Philistines came), and Caphtorim. 
013 Canaan fathered Sidon his firstborn and Heth, 
014 and the Jebusites, the Amorites, the Girgashites, 
015 the Hivites, the Arkites, the Sinites, 
016 the Arvadites, the Zemarites, and the Hamathites. 
017 The sons of Shem: Elam, Asshur, Arpachshad, Lud, and Aram. And the sons of Aram: Uz, Hul, Gether, and Meshech. 
018 Arpachshad fathered Shelah, and Shelah fathered Eber. 
019 To Eber were born two sons: the name of the one was Peleg (for in his days the earth was divided), and his brother's name was Joktan. 
020 Joktan fathered Almodad, Sheleph, Hazarmaveth, Jerah, 
021 Hadoram, Uzal, Diklah, 
022 Obal, Abimael, Sheba, 
023 Ophir, Havilah, and Jobab; all these were the sons of Joktan. 
024 Shem, Arpachshad, Shelah; 
025 Eber, Peleg, Reu; 
026 Serug, Nahor, Terah; 
027 Abram, that is, Abraham. 
028 The sons of Abraham: Isaac and Ishmael. 
029 These are their genealogies: the firstborn of Ishmael, Nebaioth, and Kedar, Adbeel, Mibsam, 
030 Mishma, Dumah, Massa, Hadad, Tema, 
031 Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah. These are the sons of Ishmael. 
032 The sons of Keturah, Abraham's concubine: she bore Zimran, Jokshan, Medan, Midian, Ishbak, and Shuah. The sons of Jokshan: Sheba and Dedan. 
033 The sons of Midian: Ephah, Epher, Hanoch, Abida, and Eldaah. All these were the descendants of Keturah. 
034 Abraham fathered Isaac. The sons of Isaac: Esau and Israel. 
035 The sons of Esau: Eliphaz, Reuel, Jeush, Jalam, and Korah. 
036 The sons of Eliphaz: Teman, Omar, Zepho, Gatam, Kenaz, and of Timna, Amalek. 
037 The sons of Reuel: Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah. 
038 The sons of Seir: Lotan, Shobal, Zibeon, Anah, Dishon, Ezer, and Dishan. 
039 The sons of Lotan: Hori and Hemam; and Lotan's sister was Timna. 
040 The sons of Shobal: Alvan, Manahath, Ebal, Shepho, and Onam. The sons of Zibeon: Aiah and Anah. 
041 The son of Anah: Dishon. The sons of Dishon: Hemdan, Eshban, Ithran, and Cheran. 
042 The sons of Ezer: Bilhan, Zaavan, and Akan. The sons of Dishan: Uz and Aran. 
043 These are the kings who reigned in the land of Edom before any king reigned over the people of Israel: Bela the son of Beor, the name of his city being Dinhabah. 
044 Bela died, and Jobab the son of Zerah of Bozrah reigned in his place. 
045 Jobab died, and Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in his place. 
046 Husham died, and Hadad the son of Bedad, who defeated Midian in the country of Moab, reigned in his place, the name of his city being Avith. 
047 Hadad died, and Samlah of Masrekah reigned in his place. 
048 Samlah died, and Shaul of Rehoboth on the Euphrates reigned in his place. 
049 Shaul died, and Baal-hanan, the son of Achbor, reigned in his place. 
050 Baal-hanan died, and Hadad reigned in his place, the name of his city being Pai; and his wife's name was Mehetabel, the daughter of Matred, the daughter of Mezahab. 
051 And Hadad died. The chiefs of Edom were: chiefs Timna, Alvah, Jetheth, 
052 Oholibamah, Elah, Pinon, 
053 Kenaz, Teman, Mibzar, 
054 Magdiel, and Iram; these are the chiefs of Edom.
