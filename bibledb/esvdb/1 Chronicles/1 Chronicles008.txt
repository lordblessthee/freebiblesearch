001 Benjamin fathered Bela his firstborn, Ashbel the second, Aharah the third, 
002 Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth. 
003 And Bela had sons: Addar, Gera, Abihud, 
004 Abishua, Naaman, Ahoah, 
005 Gera, Shephu-phan, and Huram. 
006 These are the sons of Ehud (they were heads of fathers' houses of the inhabitants of Geba, and they were carried into exile to Manahath): 
007 Naaman, Ahijah, and Gera, that is, Heglam, who fathered Uzza and Ahihud. 
008 And Shaharaim fathered sons in the country of Moab after he had sent away Hushim and Baara his wives. 
009 He fathered sons by Hodesh his wife: Jobab, Zibia, Mesha, Malcam, 
010 Jeuz, Sachia, and Mirmah. These were his sons, heads of fathers' houses. 
011 He also fathered sons by Hushim: Abitub and Elpaal. 
012 The sons of Elpaal: Eber, Misham, and Shemed, who built Ono and Lod with its towns, 
013 and Beriah and Shema (they were heads of fathers' houses of the inhabitants of Aijalon, who caused the inhabitants of Gath to flee); 
014 and Ahio, Shashak, and Jeremoth. 
015 Zebadiah, Arad, Eder, 
016 Michael, Ishpah, and Joha were sons of Beriah. 
017 Zebadiah, Meshullam, Hizki, Heber, 
018 Ishmerai, Izliah, and Jobab were the sons of Elpaal. 
019 Jakim, Zichri, Zabdi, 
020 Elienai, Zillethai, Eliel, 
021 Adaiah, Beraiah, and Shimrath were the sons of Shimei. 
022 Ishpan, Eber, Eliel, 
023 Abdon, Zichri, Hanan, 
024 Hananiah, Elam, Anthothijah, 
025 Iphdeiah, and Penuel were the sons of Shashak. 
026 Shamsherai, Shehariah, Athaliah, 
027 Jaareshiah, Elijah, and Zichri were the sons of Jeroham. 
028 These were the heads of fathers' houses, according to their generations, chief men. These lived in Jerusalem. 
029 Jeiel the father of Gibeon lived in Gibeon, and the name of his wife was Maacah. 
030 His firstborn son: Abdon, then Zur, Kish, Baal, Nadab, 
031 Gedor, Ahio, Zecher, 
032 and Mikloth (he fathered Shimeah). Now these also lived opposite their kinsmen in Jerusalem, with their kinsmen. 
033 Ner was the father of Kish, Kish of Saul, Saul of Jonathan, Malchi-shua, Abinadab and Eshbaal; 
034 and the son of Jonathan was Merib-baal; and Merib-baal was the father of Micah. 
035 The sons of Micah: Pithon, Melech, Tarea, and Ahaz. 
036 Ahaz fathered Jehoaddah, and Jehoaddah fathered Alemeth, Azmaveth, and Zimri. Zimri fathered Moza. 
037 Moza fathered Binea; Raphah was his son, Eleasah his son, Azel his son. 
038 Azel had six sons, and these are their names: Azrikam, Bocheru, Ishmael, Sheariah, Obadiah, and Hanan. All these were the sons of Azel. 
039 The sons of Eshek his brother: Ulam his firstborn, Jeush the second, and Eliphelet the third. 
040 The sons of Ulam were men who were mighty warriors, bowmen, having many sons and grandsons, 150. All these were Benjaminites.
