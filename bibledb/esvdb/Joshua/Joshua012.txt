001 Now these are the kings of the land whom the people of Israel defeated and took possession of their land beyond the Jordan toward the sunrise, from the Valley of the Arnon to Mount Hermon, with all the Arabah eastward: 
002 Sihon king of the Amorites who lived at Heshbon and ruled from Aroer, which is on the edge of the Valley of the Arnon, and from the middle of the valley as far as the river Jabbok, the boundary of the Ammonites, that is, half of Gilead, 
003 and the Arabah to the Sea of Chinneroth eastward, and in the direction of Bethjeshimoth, to the Sea of the Arabah, the Salt Sea, southward to the foot of the slopes of Pisgah; 
004 and Og king of Bashan, one of the remnant of the Rephaim, who lived at Ashtaroth and at Edrei 
005 and ruled over Mount Hermon and Salecah and all Bashan to the boundary of the Geshurites and the Maacathites, and over half of Gilead to the boundary of Sihon king of Heshbon. 
006 Moses, the servant of the LORD, and the people of Israel defeated them. And Moses the servant of the LORD gave their land for a possession to the Reubenites and the Gadites and the half-tribe of Manasseh. 
007 And these are the kings of the land whom Joshua and the people of Israel defeated on the west side of the Jordan, from Baal-gad in the Valley of Lebanon to Mount Halak, that rises toward Seir (and Joshua gave their land to the tribes of Israel as a possession according to their allotments, 
008 in the hill country, in the lowland, in the Arabah, in the slopes, in the wilderness, and in the Negeb, the land of the Hittites, the Amorites, the Canaanites, the Perizzites, the Hivites, and the Jebusites): 
009 the king of Jericho, one; the king of Ai, which is beside Bethel, one; 
010 the king of Jerusalem, one; the king of Hebron, one; 
011 the king of Jarmuth, one; the king of Lachish, one; 
012 the king of Eglon, one; the king of Gezer, one; 
013 the king of Debir, one; the king of Geder, one; 
014 the king of Hormah, one; the king of Arad, one; 
015 the king of Libnah, one; the king of Adullam, one; 
016 the king of Makkedah, one; the king of Bethel, one; 
017 the king of Tappuah, one; the king of Hepher, one; 
018 the king of Aphek, one; the king of Lasharon, one; 
019 the king of Madon, one; the king of Hazor, one; 
020 the king of Shimron-meron, one; the king of Achshaph, one; 
021 the king of Taanach, one; the king of Megiddo, one; 
022 the king of Kedesh, one; the king of Jokneam in Carmel, one; 
023 the king of Dor in Naphath-dor, one; the king of Goiim in Galilee, one; 
024 the king of Tirzah, one: in all, thirty-one kings.
