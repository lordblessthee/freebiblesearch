001 A soft answer turns away wrath, but a harsh word stirs up anger. 
002 The tongue of the wise commends knowledge, but the mouths of fools pour out folly. 
003 The eyes of the LORD are in every place, keeping watch on the evil and the good. 
004 A gentle tongue is a tree of life, but perverseness in it breaks the spirit. 
005 A fool despises his father's instruction, but whoever heeds reproof is prudent. 
006 In the house of the righteous there is much treasure, but trouble befalls the income of the wicked. 
007 The lips of the wise spread knowledge; not so the hearts of fools. 
008 The sacrifice of the wicked is an abomination to the LORD, but the prayer of the upright is acceptable to him. 
009 The way of the wicked is an abomination to the LORD, but he loves him who pursues righteousness. 
010 There is severe discipline for him who forsakes the way; whoever hates reproof will die. 
011 Sheol and Abaddon lie open before the LORD; how much more the hearts of the children of man! 
012 A scoffer does not like to be reproved; he will not go to the wise. 
013 A glad heart makes a cheerful face, but by sorrow of heart the spirit is crushed. 
014 The heart of him who has understanding seeks knowledge, but the mouths of fools feed on folly. 
015 All the days of the afflicted are evil, but the cheerful of heart has a continual feast. 
016 Better is a little with the fear of the LORD than great treasure and trouble with it. 
017 Better is a dinner of herbs where love is than a fattened ox and hatred with it. 
018 A hot-tempered man stirs up strife, but he who is slow to anger quiets contention. 
019 The way of a sluggard is like a hedge of thorns, but the path of the upright is a level highway. 
020 A wise son makes a glad father, but a foolish man despises his mother. 
021 Folly is a joy to him who lacks sense, but a man of understanding walks straight ahead. 
022 Without counsel plans fail, but with many advisers they succeed. 
023 To make an apt answer is a joy to a man, and a word in season, how good it is! 
024 The path of life leads upward for the prudent, that he may turn away from Sheol beneath. 
025 The LORD tears down the house of the proud but maintains the widow's boundaries. 
026 The thoughts of the wicked are an abomination to the LORD, but gracious words are pure. 
027 Whoever is greedy for unjust gain troubles his own household, but he who hates bribes will live. 
028 The heart of the righteous ponders how to answer, but the mouth of the wicked pours out evil things. 
029 The LORD is far from the wicked, but he hears the prayer of the righteous. 
030 The light of the eyes rejoices the heart, and good news refreshes the bones. 
031 The ear that listens to life-giving reproof will dwell among the wise. 
032 Whoever ignores instruction despises himself, but he who listens to reproof gains intelligence. 
033 The fear of the LORD is instruction in wisdom, and humility comes before honor.
