001 Whoever loves discipline loves knowledge, but he who hates reproof is stupid. 
002 A good man obtains favor from the LORD, but a man of evil devices he condemns. 
003 No one is established by wickedness, but the root of the righteous will never be moved. 
004 An excellent wife is the crown of her husband, but she who brings shame is like rottenness in his bones. 
005 The thoughts of the righteous are just; the counsels of the wicked are deceitful. 
006 The words of the wicked lie in wait for blood, but the mouth of the upright delivers them. 
007 The wicked are overthrown and are no more, but the house of the righteous will stand. 
008 A man is commended according to his good sense, but one of twisted mind is despised. 
009 Better to be lowly and have a servant than to play the great man and lack bread. 
010 Whoever is righteous has regard for the life of his beast, but the mercy of the wicked is cruel. 
011 Whoever works his land will have plenty of bread, but he who follows worthless pursuits lacks sense. 
012 Whoever is wicked covets the spoil of evildoers, but the root of the righteous bears fruit. 
013 An evil man is ensnared by the transgression of his lips, but the righteous escapes from trouble. 
014 From the fruit of his mouth a man is satisfied with good, and the work of a man's hand comes back to him. 
015 The way of a fool is right in his own eyes, but a wise man listens to advice. 
016 The vexation of a fool is known at once, but the prudent ignores an insult. 
017 Whoever speaks the truth gives honest evidence, but a false witness utters deceit. 
018 There is one whose rash words are like sword thrusts, but the tongue of the wise brings healing. 
019 Truthful lips endure forever, but a lying tongue is but for a moment. 
020 Deceit is in the heart of those who devise evil, but those who plan peace have joy. 
021 No ill befalls the righteous, but the wicked are filled with trouble. 
022 Lying lips are an abomination to the LORD, but those who act faithfully are his delight. 
023 A prudent man conceals knowledge, but the heart of fools proclaims folly. 
024 The hand of the diligent will rule, while the slothful will be put to forced labor. 
025 Anxiety in a man's heart weighs him down, but a good word makes him glad. 
026 One who is righteous is a guide to his neighbor, but the way of the wicked leads them astray. 
027 Whoever is slothful will not roast his game, but the diligent man will get precious wealth. 
028 In the path of righteousness is life, and in its pathway there is no death.
